Scenerio

You are auditioning for a new T.V show called "So you want to be famous?", despite your lack of talent you figure why not! You don't have to be talented to be famous, right?


While waiting for your turn to audition, you notice your opponent practicing a flawless dance routine. You look at their audition number and realize they are going AFTER you. Shall you steal their whole routine(choice1)? Or stick with your unrehearsed and improvised audition(choice2)? Each choice has different consequences, but only one will lead you to fame!

What Will You Choose?
